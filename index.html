Tourist Safety Guidelines & Security Components

Tourist Safety Guidelines

Emergency Contacts

Save local emergency numbers: Police (113), Ambulance (118), and your country's embassy contact information.

Document Security

Keep digital copies of your passport, ID, and travel documents. Use hotel safes for original documents.

Local Customs

Research dress codes and cultural norms. Respect religious sites and local traditions to avoid issues.

Health Precautions

Check if vaccinations are required. Pack a basic medical kit and know locations of nearby hospitals.

Security Components

Location Sharing

Enable real-time location sharing with trusted contacts. Set check-in times during your travels.

Digital Security

Use VPN on public Wi-Fi. Enable two-factor authentication on important accounts.

Money Protection

Use RFID-blocking wallets. Split cash and cards between different storage locations.

Transport Safety

Use licensed taxis and rideshares. Share ride details with someone you trust.
