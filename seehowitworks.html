One-Touch Emergency Alert System

Our emergency alert system is designed to get you help instantly when you need it most. Here's how it works:

Press and hold the emergency button for 3 seconds
The system automatically captures your precise location
Your emergency profile is sent to local authorities
Your designated emergency contacts are notified
Real-time updates are shared until the situation is resolved
